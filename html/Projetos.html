Projects Against Racism:



Baobab Fund:


The Baobá Fund for Racial Equity is a non-profit organization created in 2011. Its objective is to mobilize people and resources, both in Brazil and abroad, to support projects and actions that promote racial equity for the black population. They are pioneers and unique in dedicating themselves exclusively to this cause. Baobá finances projects and initiatives that seek social justice and racial equality. Its commitment is to humanize people through donations and mobilize society to promote significant changes1.
